Why Sync?

Sync is an open-sourced lobby-bot service that you can put to your own use.

Apart from that, Sync has built-in support for commands, meaning that you do not need to make changes to the code! Just follow the guide in the docs!

Our bots are easy-to-use and easy to set-up! All you need is to create an epic account for the bot. Adding the bot to your personal account is bannable from Fortnite.

Designed for human

We believe a good design is when it can serve the human effectively, so they can work on stuff efficiently, this is why we don't focus on making stuff that only looks cool, we focus on improving the efficiency.
